## Query 1: How do I adjust the carburetor on a Model T?

Connect the carburetor adjusting rod: insert the forked end into the head of the needle valve, secure with cotter pin. Install rod through slot in dash, insert forked end into needle valve, lock with cotter pin. (Manual-specific: rod, needle valve, throttle lever "B".)
